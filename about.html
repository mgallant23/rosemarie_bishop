About Me


Rosemarie E. Bishop was born and raised in Lackawanna, NY, married Christopher A. Bishop from Buffalo, NY in 1992, and moved to New England in 1993. In 1995 she and her husband built their “cabin in the woods” in a small town in New Hampshire where they reside to this day. Bishop began writing novels in 1999 and currently has five other novels in print, three of which make up The Moral Vampire Series – Search For A Soul, A Matter Of Conscience, and Spiritual Vengeance, plus three stand alone novels, Noah’s Garden, Mrs. Pope and A Brother's Love. For more information about this author and her work visit www.rosemariebishop.com.
